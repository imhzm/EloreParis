# Public Site Launch Gates

## Experience

- Navigation is clear on desktop and mobile.
- Primary CTA is obvious.
- Loading, empty, success, and error states are present where needed.

## Accessibility

- Keyboard access works for primary paths.
- Images and controls have usable labels.
- Contrast and focus states are not obviously broken.

## SEO and Schema

- Title and description are not placeholders.
- Canonical and robots behavior are intentional.
- Open Graph basics exist.
- Relevant schema is present for organization, product, article, FAQ, or local business surfaces.

## Analytics

- Main conversion events are defined.
- Form submit, booking, checkout, or lead steps are measurable.
- Broken links or dead forms are not silently ignored.

## Performance

- No obviously oversized media on critical paths.
- Fonts, scripts, and hero assets are reasonable.
- Mobile first paint is not degraded by avoidable client-side work.

## Release

- Build passes.
- Deployment target is known.
- Rollback or redeploy path is known.
- Monitoring or at least error visibility is in place.
