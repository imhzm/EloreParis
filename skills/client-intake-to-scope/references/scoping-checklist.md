# Scoping Checklist

## Inputs

- What is the business goal?
- Who is the target audience or operator?
- What is the main action users should take?
- What surfaces are included?

## Scope Control

- What is required in phase 1?
- What is explicitly out of scope?
- What dependencies are external to engineering?

## Acceptance

- What does success look like for the client?
- What flows must work on day one?
- What quality bar is expected: MVP, polished launch, internal tool?

## Risk

- Missing brand/content assets
- Unknown integrations
- Payment, auth, or migration complexity
- Timeline risk from too many coupled deliverables
